Music Score Creator makes it simple to create music sheets.

Music basics

Bar-lines

  • | Bar : separe the divisions of time
  • =|| Double Bar : divide one complete musical idea from another
  • =|: Repeat Begin : repeat the piece from here
  • =:| Repeat End : indicate that the music is to be repeated from a Repeat Begin Bar. If there are not, the music is repeated from the beginning
  • =:: Double Repeat : cf. repeat begin and repeat end
  • =|= End Bar : indicate that the end of the piece has been reached

Durations

  • 1 while note
  • 2 half notes
  • 4 quarter notes
  • 8 eighth notes
  • 16 sixteenth notes

tabstave notation=true tablature=false notes Cn-D-E/4 F#/5
